# Project PRD: Fluxo - Asset Intelligence & Wealth Management

## 1. Executive Summary
**Fluxo** is a high-fidelity, technical wealth management platform designed for users who demand precision, clarity, and deep analytical insights. Moving away from traditional retail banking aesthetics, Fluxo adopts a "Neon Dark" interface inspired by technical trading terminals and high-end asset management tools.

## 2. Brand Identity & Design System
*   **Brand Philosophy**: Lowercase "fluxo" represents technical integrity and market fluidity.
*   **Visual Direction**: Deep Dark Mode with high-contrast electric neon accents.
*   **Core Palette**:
    *   **Base Surface**: `#060B14` (Deep Space Blue)
    *   **Primary Accent**: `#00F5D4` (Electric Cyan) — Used for balances, brand, and primary CTAs.
    *   **Functional Colors**: Neon Lime (`#B4FF6A`) for income, Neon Rose (`#FF4D6A`) for expenses, and Neon Amber (`#FFB020`) for alerts.
*   **Typography**:
    *   **Technical Data**: *JetBrains Mono* (Light/Regular) for all numerical values and chart labels.
    *   **Interface**: *Plus Jakarta Sans* (or *Inter* based on implementation) for readable UI copy and labels.
*   **Signature Effects**: Subtle neon glows (`text-shadow` and `box-shadow`) applied to primary technical data and active states.

## 3. Product Architecture (Screen Inventory)

### 3.1. Onboarding Flow
*   **Onboarding Welcome**: A refined entry point identifying core user focus (Emergency Fund, Long-term, etc.).
*   **Open Finance Integration**: Secure connection to financial institutions.
*   **First Goal Setup**: Interactive goal definition and initial investment logic.

### 3.2. Core Management
*   **Consolidated Dashboard**: Real-time asset overview and weekly cash flow tracking.
*   **Transaction Ledger**: High-fidelity timeline with detailed filtering and spending insights.
*   **Metas (Goals)**: Progress tracking with projection charts and automated smart setups.

### 3.3. Advanced Analytics & Support
*   **Investment Deep-Dive**: Performance charts (Asset vs. Benchmark), fund composition, and top holdings.
*   **Financial Reports**: Multi-layered asset allocation and volatility indexing.
*   **Security Center**: 2FA management, session tracking, and privacy controls.
*   **Help & Support**: Integrated documentation and support channels.

## 4. Technical Specifications for Export
*   **Responsive Framework**: 12-column desktop grid transition to a streamlined Mobile Bottom-Nav architecture.
*   **Component Architecture**: Card-based layouts with `1px solid #1E3045` borders and `8px` corner radius.
*   **Accessibility**: High-contrast neon-on-dark ensures readability in low-light environments.
