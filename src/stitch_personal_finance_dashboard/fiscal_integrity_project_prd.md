# Project PRD: Fiscal Integrity - Personal Wealth Management

## 1. Product Vision
Fiscal Integrity is a high-fidelity personal finance and wealth management platform designed to provide users with absolute clarity and control over their financial life. It bridges the gap between daily expense tracking and long-term investment strategy through an intuitive, professional interface.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **High-Net-Worth Individuals**: Requiring detailed analysis of investment performance and holdings.
*   **Goal-Oriented Savers**: Users building emergency funds or long-term wealth through automated contributions.
*   **Data-Driven Professionals**: Users who value detailed transaction history, consolidated reporting, and Open Finance integration.

## 3. Core Features & Functional Requirements

### 3.1. Onboarding & Integration
*   **Personalized Welcome**: Multi-step flow to identify user goals (Emergency Fund, Debt, Long-term, Learning).
*   **Open Finance Connection**: Secure synchronization with major financial institutions (Itaú, Nubank, Bradesco, etc.) using 256-bit encryption.
*   **Goal Initialization**: Interactive setup for the first savings goal with automatic monthly investment calculation.

### 3.2. Financial Dashboard & Tracking
*   **Consolidated Dashboard**: Real-time overview of assets, liabilities, and net worth.
*   **Detailed Transactions**: Robust ledger with advanced filtering (period, category, account) and export capabilities (CSV/PDF).
*   **Consolidated Reporting**: Analytical views of financial health and progress over time.

### 3.3. Goal Management
*   **Savings Goals**: Dedicated interface for creating and monitoring specific financial targets.
*   **Automated Contributions**: Intelligent scheduling of deposits to ensure goals are met within the target timeframe.
*   **Redemption Flow**: Secure process for withdrawing funds from specific goals.

### 3.4. Investment Intelligence
*   **Asset Deep-Dive**: Detailed view of specific funds (e.g., Global Strategic Balanced Fund) including NAV, performance charts, and fund composition (Equity vs. Fixed Income).
*   **Top Holdings**: Transparency into underlying assets (e.g., Apple, Microsoft, NVIDIA).
*   **Trade Panel**: Integrated Buy/Sell module for immediate action within the asset context.

### 3.5. Security & Support
*   **Security Center**: 2FA management, active session monitoring (device tracking), and password controls.
*   **Privacy Controls**: Data export (JSON/CSV) and visibility settings.
*   **Help Center**: Smart FAQ search, ticket management, and direct support channels (Live Chat/Email).

## 4. Visual Identity & Design System (Fiscal Integrity)
*   **Design Principle**: Clarity, Trust, and Professionalism.
*   **Typography**: Plus Jakarta Sans (Modern, clean, highly readable).
*   **Color Palette**: Professional deep blues (`#0f172a`), emerald greens for growth/success, and a clean light surface (`#f7f9fb`).
*   **Layout**: Generous whitespace, card-based information architecture, and a fixed side navigation for rapid context switching.
