05.

Brand Philosophy

Lowercase ‘fluxo’ Integrity

The brand name ‘fluxo’ must always be rendered in lowercase. This represents a lack of ego in the face of financial data and emphasizes the fluidity of the markets. It is a technical mark, not a corporate scream.

The Luminous Depth Principle

Avoid traditional drop shadows. In the deep dark environment of Fluxo, depth is created through tonal layering (#060B14 to #152236) and the use of 1px "ghost borders" (#1E3045). Neon glows are reserved only for primary actions and active data monitoring.

Numerical Presentation

All primary currency figures must use JetBrains Mono with a subtle text-glow effect. This mimics high-end trading terminals and ensures legibility in low-light environments.
